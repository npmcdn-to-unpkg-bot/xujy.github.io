Meet our Mission and our Team.

We are a group of Notre Dame sophomomores with a passion for design. As members of Design for America we created The Digital Dorm Room to help incoming First Years reduce anxiety and prepare for college by giving them a taste of dorm life. To do this, we put panoramas from the tour.nd.edu website all in one place. We believe strongly that in an age of rapid environmental degradation, the sustainable dorm room is a must in creating socially responsible citizens. We've added some recommended products and tips to get you on your way to a lifetime of good choices.
